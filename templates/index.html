Random Forest trees and Gradient Boosting work fine as well with precition of >94-97%.

To our surprise, classical MLP classifier in keras performed much worse than other non-DL ML algorithms, giving just 60% precition The only way to boost MLP sequential classifier was to use One Shot Learning add-on. Keras one shot learning Although in the example above it was used with LSTM, the usage of the regulizer, resulted in 30% precition increase for our case.
